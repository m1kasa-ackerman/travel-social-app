Document 1: Product Requirements Document (PRD)
1. Executive Summary
A social-first travel platform where users share visual itineraries and restaurant reviews that others can instantly save to an interactive, personal map.

2. Target Audience

The Explorer: Independent travelers looking for non-generic, peer-vetted spots.

The Foodie: Users seeking authentic restaurant recommendations with geographical context.

3. Functional Requirements

Itinerary Engine: Multi-day post format with linked "Points of Interest" (POIs).

One-Tap Save: "Wishlist" button on any post that auto-populates the user’s map.

Dynamic Maps: Map view showing personal saved places vs. community trending spots.

Follow System: Social feed based on followed travelers and local experts.

4. User Experience (UX) Goals

Minimize the "Copy-Paste" loop (No more manual entry into Google Maps).

High-fidelity visual storytelling (Minimalist, Dribbble-inspired UI).
